Meet Ms. Queen Onuoha

Kween4bic Beauty World is proudly led by Ms. Queen Onuoha - a gifted beauty entrepreneur with a gentle eye for detail and a genuine love for helping women glow from the inside out. She believes beauty should never feel rushed or impersonal; it should feel thoughtful, feminine, and full of care.

With a Bachelor's Degree in Architecture from the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, Queen brings a designer's sense of balance, structure, and artistry into every hairstyle, nail set, and treatment. That creative foundation shows in the clean lines, elegant finishes, and beautifully finished looks her clients adore.

From luxury wigs and scalp-loving hair treatments to statement nails, soothing facials, and pampering manicure & pedicure sessions - you receive a tailored experience built around your face shape, lifestyle, and personal style. Many clients say they leave not only looking better, but feeling lighter, happier, and more confident.
